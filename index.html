a11y myths

Debunked

Accessibility only affects a small group of users

Not really. Around 15% of the world's population, or estimated 1 billion people, live with disabilities, they are the world's largest minority. The number of people with disability is dramatically increasing.

More details about Accessibility only affects a small group of users

Disabled users don't use my website

How can you be so sure? Many people with disabilities like color blindness, limited motor skills, etc. use websites just like other users. Also, many assistive technologies are not detectable in any way.
